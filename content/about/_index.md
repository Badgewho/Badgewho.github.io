## 🔬 研究兴趣 | Research Interests

我目前主要关注以下几个方向：  
My current research mainly focuses on the following directions:

1. **计算病理 / Computational Pathology**：围绕乳腺癌病理诊断、全视野病理图像（WSI）分类与病理形态建模，研究弱监督学习、多实例学习与特征重嵌入方法。  
   I work on breast cancer pathology diagnosis, whole-slide image classification, and histomorphology modeling, with an emphasis on weakly supervised learning, multiple instance learning, and feature re-embedding.

2. **医学影像分析 / Medical Image Analysis**：结合模式识别与深度学习，探索知识蒸馏、不确定性建模以及跨层级特征整合。  
   I study medical image analysis by combining pattern recognition and deep learning, especially knowledge distillation, uncertainty modeling, and cross-level feature integration.

3. **多模态智能 / Multimodal Intelligence**：融合病理图像、基因表达等多源信息，服务于可解释癌症生存分析与精准医疗任务。  
   I am also interested in multimodal learning that integrates pathology images and gene expression for interpretable cancer survival analysis and precision oncology.
